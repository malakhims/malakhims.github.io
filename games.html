Game Development

I've been developing small indie games since I was in high school! I love surreal and psychological horror. My upcoming game is in 3D!

Mall

Mall is a Yume Nikki inspired interactive web page created for n online zine. My ultimate desire for this project was to prove that I could make something like this with only front end tools. I collaborated a friend on the graphics for the project, and he also provided a tune for the page.

It was a success, and is actually upload on this website.

Meat Girl

Meat Girl was created for the 2024 Frigid Cold Jam. It was made from October to December (a time span of about two months).

I was the sole developer and artist. All music is Creative Commons.

Ommatophilia


Ommatophilia was worked on from 2021 to December of 2025. It is a surreal game focused on abuse and the end of adolescence.

I am currently working on a definitive edition I hope to release on more platforms. The 2025 release was a delight to finally give to the world, but since it was worked on for four years, I felt as though I could improve.
